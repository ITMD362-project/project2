Body of Work: Yale School of Art MFA 2020

Published 2021

Body of Work showcases the collected work of the graduating MFA class of 2020, and marks the fourth installment of Yale School of Art's all-school MFA book series. Featuring an afterword by Stavros Niarchos Foundation Dean and Professor of Art Marta Kuzma.

Contributors: Steven Rodriguez and Nicholas Weltyk (Graphic Design '20), Carly Sheehan (Painting/Printmaking '20), Angela Chen (Photography '20), David Roy (Sculpture '20)

Edited by: Willis Kingery (Graphic Design '19)

Designed by: Bryant Wells (Graphic Design '20)

Cover illustrations by: Timothy Brawner (Painting/Printmaking '20)

Body of Work: Yale School of Art MFA 2019

Published 2020

Featuring a foreword by Stavros Niarchos Foundation Dean Marta Kuzma, Body of Work showcases the collected work of the graduating MFA class of 2019, and marks the third installment of Yale School of Art's all-school MFA book series.

Contributors: Emma Gregoline (Graphic Design '19), Emile Mausner (Painting/Printmaking '19), Genesis Báez (Photography '19), Alfredo Diaz (Sculpture '19)

Edited by: Willis Kingery (Graphic Design '19), Edi Dai (Painting/Printmaking '19), David Knowles (Graphic Design '19)

Designed by: David Knowles and Willis Kingery (Graphic Design '19)

Yale School of Art MFA 2018

Published 2019

Featuring a foreword by Dean Marta Kuzma, this publication showcases the collected work of the graduating MFA class of 2018, and marks the second installment of Yale School of Art's all-school MFA book series.

Contributors: Bryce Wilner (Graphic Design '18), Ernest A. Bryant III (Painting/Printmaking '18), Ian Page (Sculpture '18)

Edited by: Ayham Ghraowi (Graphic Design '17)

Designed by: Hrefna Sigurðardóttir (Graphic Design '18)

Cover image: Dan Swindel (Photography '18), Untitled (2019)
